Kindergarten Wing – Joyful Beginnings

Welcome to Our Tiny Learners’ World

At Wisdom Public School, kindergarten is a place where curiosity shines, creativity blossoms, and confidence grows.

Every day, our little explorers step into a vibrant environment filled with love, learning, and laughter. Our Early Year Program is designed to nurture holistic development through hands-on experiences, interactive learning and joyful play.

OUR LEARNING APPROACH

  • Ask Questions and explore new ideas
  • Develop language, numeracy and communication skills
  • Build self confidence and independence
  • Work together, share and develop social values

A DAY IN KINDERGARTEN JOURNEY

Follow our little ones through a day of joy, learning, and growth.

09:00 AM

Morning Assembly

We start our day with gratitude, prayers, and a little warm-up exercise to get the energy flowing!

10:30 AM

Creative Hands Time

Exploring textures and colors! Our students develop motor skills through painting, crafting, and creative play.

12:00 PM

Healthy Snack Time

A time to refuel! Kids learn table manners and the importance of sharing healthy meals together.

01:30 PM

Storytelling & Fun

We wrap up the day with magical stories and fun games that spark imagination before heading home.
