Our Early Literacy and Numeracy sessions introduce children to letters, sounds, numbers, and basic mathematical concepts in a fun, engaging, and pressure-free environment.

Learning Objectives

  • Recognize letters, sounds, and simple words
  • Develop early reading and writing readiness
  • Understand numbers, counting, and patterns
  • Build logical thinking and problem-solving skills

What Children Learn

Early Literacy

Phonics, letter recognition, storytelling, picture reading, and vocabulary building through songs and stories.

Early Numeracy

Counting, number recognition, shapes, patterns, comparisons, and simple problem-solving using real-life objects.
